# Sample Company Policy

> This is a self-written demo document for RAG testing. It is not copied from any real company's internal policy.

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains basic conduct rules for the demo commerce company used by the Enterprise Multi-Tool Agent Platform. It covers conflicts of interest, data security, approval workflows, and customer complaint handling.

## 2. Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any situation where personal interests may conflict with company responsibilities. Examples include vendor relationships, family-owned suppliers, side businesses that sell similar products, or personal benefits offered by partners.

When a conflict may exist, the employee should stop making related business decisions, notify the direct manager and compliance owner, submit a written disclosure, and wait for approval before continuing the activity.

## 3. Human Approval Requirements

Human approval is required when an action may create business, compliance, or customer risk. Approval is required for sending customer-facing messages generated by an AI agent, exporting order or after-sales data, changing refund decisions for high-value orders, overriding SQL Guardrails, or sharing reports outside the approved internal team.

AI-generated email drafts must be reviewed by a human approver before any external communication is sent.

## 4. Data Security Rules

The following data must not be sent outside approved systems:

- API keys, tokens, passwords, cookies, and private credentials.
- Full customer addresses, phone numbers, or payment data.
- Internal database connection strings.
- Personal employee records.
- Raw customer complaint records that identify a person.

Reports should use aggregated metrics whenever possible. If examples are needed, they must be anonymized.

## 5. Customer Complaint Handling

Customer complaints should be handled with clear ownership and traceability. The support team should classify the issue, check order history, review after-sales policy, and record the decision in the audit log.

Complaints should be escalated when the customer reports product safety risk, the order value is unusually high, the issue involves repeated delivery failure, the customer threatens legal or regulatory action, or the proposed resolution needs a manual exception.

## 6. AI Agent Usage

The AI Agent may assist with knowledge retrieval, SQL analytics, report drafting, and tool execution. It must not bypass role permissions, SQL Guardrails, audit logging, or human approval requirements.
